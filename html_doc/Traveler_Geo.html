Passive Talents
Shattered Darkrock
Reduces Starfell Sword's CD by 2s.
Frenzied Rockslide
The final hit of a Normal Attack combo triggers a collapse, dealing 60% of ATK as AoE Geo DMG.
Constellations
Invincible Stonewall
Allies within the radius of Wake of Earth have their CRIT Rate increased by 10% and have increased resistance against interruption.
Rockcore Meltdown
When the meteorite created by Starfell Sword is destroyed, it will also explode, dealing additional AoE Geo DMG equal to the amount of damage dealt by Starfell Sword.
Will of the Rock
Increases the Level of Wake of Earth by 3.
Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Reaction Force
The shockwave triggered by Wake of Earth regenerates 5 Energy for every enemy hit. A maximum of 25 Energy can be regenerated in this manner at any one time.
Meteorite Impact
Increases the Level of Starfell Sword by 3.
Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Everlasting Boulder
The barrier created by Wake of Earth lasts 5s longer. The meteorite created by Starfell Sword lasts 10s longer.
